# Study Guides

These study guides are exam worksheets that are designed to mimic a quiz or
midterm in the class. They are divided along certain topics, not necessarily
arranged chronologically like your midterms might.

In each classes topic directory, there are two PDFs. One is blank, and there is
space left under each question so that you can print out the sheet and attempt
answering the worksheet as you might in a quiz. The second PDF is a key. Lots
of the questions have multiple valid interpretations, and I just gave my own
interpretation. It's up to you to judge whether or not your answers are valid;
the key is just there for a sanity check.
